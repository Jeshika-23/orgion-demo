Our Story

Founded in 2025, Orgion began with a simple belief: that nature holds the secret to true radiance. What started as a small kitchen experiment with lavender from our backyard has grown into a community properly dedicated to organic, sustainable skincare.

We believe that what you put on your skin should be as pure as what you put in your body. That's why every soap, oil, and shampoo we create is handcrafted in small batches using only the finest ethically sourced ingredients. No parabens, no sulfates, just pure botanical goodness.

Our Promise

Cruelty-Free • Sustainable • 100% Organic

From our biodegradable packaging to our carbon-neutral shipping, we are committed to protecting the planet that provides us with these healing ingredients. Thank you for joining us on this journey to a more natural, glowing you.
